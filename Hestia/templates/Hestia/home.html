About

Parents are in the best position to create a healthy environment for their children to learn and grow in. Unfortunately, when it comes to childhood mental and behavioral conditions, there is still a lot of misunderstanding, lack of resources, and feeling of helplessness among parents. The purpose of our website is to integrate and make resources more accessible by providing educational videos and online support groups for these childhood conditions.
